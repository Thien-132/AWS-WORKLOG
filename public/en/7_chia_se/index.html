7. Sharing and Feedback

General Assessment

1. Working Environment

During my internship at FCAJ, I felt the working environment was very comfortable and friendly. Everyone in the team was ready to help when I faced difficulties, especially when I was new to the job. The working atmosphere is serious but does not create pressure, helping me easily integrate and focus on completing assigned tasks.

2. Mentor / Team Admin Support

My mentor guided me very enthusiastically throughout the internship. Whenever I encountered an error or didn’t understand an issue, my mentor explained it thoroughly and guided me on how to find the solution instead of just giving the answer. The team admin also provided full support regarding documents, information, and matters related to the internship, making my work much smoother.

3. Relevance to the Major

The tasks assigned to me were related to the Information Technology major I am studying. In addition to the knowledge learned at school, I also had access to many new technologies and practical work processes such as Git, GitHub, AWS, Hugo, and teamwork coordination. These are very useful experiences for me.

4. Opportunities for Learning and Skill Development

The internship helped me learn many new things, not only in terms of professional knowledge but also working skills. I practiced skills in self-researching documentation, problem-solving, teamwork, and managing work progress. Through this, I also gained more confidence when approaching new technologies.

5. Culture and Team Spirit

I highly appreciate the working spirit of everyone in the company. Senior colleagues were always ready to support and share their experiences when I needed them. Everyone works very professionally but is still approachable and friendly, which made me feel comfortable throughout the internship.

6. Policies and Support for Interns

During the internship, the company provided conditions for me to have an environment to learn and develop. Besides receiving professional guidance, I also participated in workshops and practical knowledge-sharing sessions. This was an opportunity to broaden my understanding and accumulate more experiences.
