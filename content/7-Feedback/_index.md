## General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**
The working environment at AWS Vietnam is extremely friendly and open. Team members are always willing to assist when I face difficulties, even outside of working hours. The dynamic workspace helps me focus better. However, I think organizing more team bonding sessions would help everyone connect more closely.

**2. Mentor & Admin Support**
My mentors provided detailed guidance, explained complex concepts clearly, and consistently encouraged me to ask questions. The admin team facilitated all procedures and provided excellent documentation. I highly appreciate that my mentors allowed me to experiment and troubleshoot issues independently rather than simply giving me the answers.

**3. Alignment with Academic Major**
My role as an FCJ Cloud Intern aligns perfectly with my Information Technology major at HUTECH. Getting hands-on experience with AWS Serverless architectures and AI integration not only reinforced my foundational knowledge but also expanded my practical skillset significantly.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**
During this internship, I acquired new skills such as deploying cloud infrastructure (AWS CDK), working effectively in a team, and communicating professionally within a multinational tech company. My mentors also shared practical industry insights that helped me shape my career path as a cloud engineer.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**
The company culture is highly positive: mutual respect, serious work ethic, yet very approachable. Whenever a challenging project or lab emerged, everyone collaborated and supported each other regardless of their position. This made me feel like an integral part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Policies & Intern Benefits**
The company provides a highly professional internship environment with flexible working hours. Being granted an AWS account for direct hands-on practice and participating in internal training sessions were massive advantages.

## Additional Questions

* **What were you most satisfied with during your internship?**
  The dynamic working environment, the opportunity to work directly with core AWS technologies, and the enthusiastic support from industry experts.
* **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**
  It would be great to include more simulated system failure scenarios (troubleshooting labs) during practical sessions to help interns sharpen their real-world incident response skills.
* **Would you recommend your friends to intern here? Why?**
  Absolutely. It is a golden opportunity for IT students to gain exposure to cloud computing with world-class technology standards.
